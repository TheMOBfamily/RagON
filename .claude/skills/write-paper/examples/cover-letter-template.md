# Cover Letter Template

> Template cho thu gui bien tap tap chi.


## Template (English)

```
Dear Editor-in-Chief / Editorial Board,

We are pleased to submit our manuscript titled "{PAPER TITLE}" for consideration for publication in {JOURNAL NAME}.

**CONTRIBUTION:**
{1-2 sentences describing the main contribution and novelty of the paper.}

**RELEVANCE:**
{1-2 sentences explaining why this paper fits the journal's scope and audience.}

**KEY FINDINGS:**
{2-3 bullet points summarizing the main findings.}

**CONFIRMATION:**
- This manuscript has not been published and is not under consideration elsewhere.
- All authors have approved the manuscript and agree with submission.
- [If applicable] This study was approved by {IRB/Ethics Committee}.
- [If applicable] Data and code are available at {DOI/URL}.

**SUGGESTED REVIEWERS (Optional):**
1. {Name, Affiliation, Email} - Reason: {expertise in X}
2. {Name, Affiliation, Email} - Reason: {expertise in Y}

**EXCLUDED REVIEWERS (Optional):**
- {Name} - Reason: {conflict of interest}

We believe this paper makes a significant contribution to {field} and would be of interest to your readers.

Thank you for your consideration.

Sincerely,

{Corresponding Author Name}
{Title, Affiliation}
{Email}
{ORCID}

On behalf of all authors:
{Author 2 Name, Affiliation}
{Author 3 Name, Affiliation}
```

{1-2 cau giai thich tai sao bai bao phu hop voi pham vi tap chi.}

## Key Points

| Element | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Length | 1 page maximum |
| Tone | Professional, concise |
| Focus | Contribution + Fit + Confirmation |
| Avoid | Excessive praise, lengthy background |


## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Too long | Keep to 1 page |
| Generic | Customize for each journal |
| No contribution | State clearly what's new |
| Missing confirmations | Include all required statements |
| Wrong journal name | Double-check before sending |
